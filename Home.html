Get to know Surigao

"Discover Surigao, where stunning beaches and vibrant landscapes offer the perfect getaway. Book your next adventure now and experience the beauty and tranquility that only Surigao can provide!"













Surigao, located in the northeastern tip of Mindanao, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty and rich culture. It is a gateway to the world-famous Siargao Island, the surfing capital of the Philippines, and home to unique attractions like the Enchanted River and the Sohoton Cove. With its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and captivating caves, Surigao offers endless adventures for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike. Discover a perfect blend of relaxation and excitement in this tropical paradise!

1. Cabgan Island

Barobo, Surigao del Sur

Cabgan Island is a 20 to 30-minute boat ride from the boat port of Barobo at the back of old public market. The sea is calm at around four in the afternoon we head our way to the island.

2. Panaraga Island

Barobo, Surigao del Sur

Panaraga Island Resort in Surigao delights visitors with its secluded beaches, crystal-clear waters, and tranquil ambiance, offering the perfect retreat for relaxation and water activities.

3. Vanishing Island

Barobo, Surigao del Sur

Turtle Island in Surigao captivates visitors with its unspoiled beaches, lush landscapes, and abundant sea turtles, providing a peaceful sanctuary for nature enthusiasts and eco-tourists.
